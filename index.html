Introduction

In today's digital age, cybersecurity and cyber awareness have become critical for protecting personal information, corporate data, and national security. This website aims to provide essential information, tips, and resources to help individuals and organizations stay safe online.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practices and technologies used to protect computers, networks, programs, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, damage, or theft. It encompasses everything from antivirus software to firewalls, encryption, and multi-factor authentication.

Importance of Cyber Awareness

Cyber awareness is the understanding and recognition of potential cyber threats and the actions needed to avoid them. Being cyber aware means knowing how to identify phishing emails, using strong passwords, and keeping software updated to prevent vulnerabilities.
